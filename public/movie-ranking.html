My Top 3 Movies of All Time

MS Dhoni

This is the first ever movie that I watched in a movie theatre with my friends!
The movie is about the bio-pic of captain cool MSD, which is ery much motivational to watch.

Super 30

This is a movie portraying the struggle of a small village boy, Anand Kumar and how he mentored some of the unprivilleged students to get into the IITs.

Chichhore

This movie is a reflection of college life. It is full of fun to watch. It is one of my most favourite movies!
